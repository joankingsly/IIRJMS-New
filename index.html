About Us

The Indian International Research Journal for Management and Social Science (IIRJMS) is a peer-reviewed academic journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research that advances knowledge in management and the social sciences. The journal welcomes empirical, theoretical, and review articles that contribute to scholarly discourse and inform practice and policy.

IIRJMS provides an international forum for researchers, academicians, practitioners, and policy makers to disseminate interdisciplinary research addressing contemporary issues in business, economics, public policy, sociology, psychology, education, and related fields.

Journal Highlights

  • Double-blind peer-reviewed
  • Bi-annual publication (January–June, July–December)
  • Open to global submissions
  • Focus on management and social science domains
  • Original, unpublished work only

Manuscript Submission

IIRJMS welcomes original, unpublished manuscripts that fall within the broad domains of management and social science. Authors are requested to carefully follow the guidelines below before submitting their work.

Author Guidelines (Sample)

  • Manuscripts should be written in clear English and follow a consistent academic style.
  • Typical length: 5,000–8,000 words including references, tables, and figures.
  • Structure: Title, Abstract (150–250 words), Keywords (3–6), Introduction, Literature Review, Methodology, Results and Discussion, Conclusion, References.
  • Referencing style: e.g. APA or other style specified by the journal (update as per IIRJMS policy).
  • Manuscripts must not be under consideration by any other journal at the time of submission.
  • All submissions will be screened for plagiarism and research ethics compliance.

Review & Publication Process

  1. Initial editorial screening for scope, originality, and basic quality.
  2. Double-blind peer review by at least two independent reviewers.
  3. Decision: acceptance, minor revision, major revision, or rejection.
  4. Revised manuscript submission (where applicable).
  5. Final acceptance and copyediting.
  6. Online/print publication in the relevant volume and issue.
